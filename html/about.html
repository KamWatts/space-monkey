    SpaceMonkeys: The Game

    Become a

    HERO OF SPACE!!!

    Do you love space? Do you love monkeys? If you answered yes, then this game is for you. SpaceMonkeys is a top down racing game which pits your assigned Monkey against a randomly selected CPU Monkey. The answers you give to each of four randomly generated questions will determine your character from a choice of four unique Monkeys. Race your Monkey around the sun and win the best of three rounds to prove to the galaxy that you are the best SpaceMonkey... The Hero of Space!!!
