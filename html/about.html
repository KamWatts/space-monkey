SpaceMonkeys: The Game

HERO OF SPACE!!!

Do you love monkeys? If you do, then this game is for you. SpaceMonkeys is a top down racing game between your assigned monkey against the CPU monkey. You have 4 types of monkeys with different speed attribute and different orbit track that can be assigned to you, depending on your answers to our 4 random generated questions. It will be best of 3 racing events and 3 laps around the sun. So come on and prove yourself to the galaxy that you have the best SpaceMonkey... The Hero of Space!!!

Oh, and one more thing... if you are lucky, you might get a SECRET MONKEY...=)
